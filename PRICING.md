## 0. The Four Layers (what everything is built from)

Every product is a combination of these four layers:

| Layer | Name | What it means | Audience |
|---|---|---|---|
| **01** | AI-**built** | Humans look at it — fast, custom, AI-made website | Everyone |
| **02** | AI-**powered** | An AI agent lives on the site and helps *your* visitors (chat, lead capture, booking) | Most clients |
| **03** | AI-**readable** | Outside AIs (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude) can *find & recommend* you | Everyone (basics included) |
| **04** | AI-**actionable** | Outside AIs can *operate* the site — book, quote, pay — with no browser. Delivered via **MCP** | Tech / SaaS |

One-line pitch: **Built by AI · Powered by AI · Found by AI · Used by AI.**

Lead sales with **interaction** (Layer 02 agent + Layer 04 / MCP — provable on the call today). Treat **visibility** (Layer 03 search citations) as the bonus that compounds over weeks-to-months — never promise instant AI citations.

| Market | Basic site | Professional/custom | Agency tier | Notes |

| Slovakia | €400–800 | €700–2,500 | €2,000–7,000 | Commodity floor at €79–499 exists |

| AEO/AI visibility | €800 (AEOScore Quick Wins) | €2,500+ | — | No CEE competitor at the wedge price |

**Three rules:**
1. **Sit at the top of the freelancer band, below the agency band** (€1,000–2,500 sweet spot). The €79-site crowd is not competition; agencies are slower and don't have the AI story.
2. **Zero case studies = the first 3 deals buy proof, not margin.** The Pilot Program (§3) is the actual launch pricing; the list below is what pilots graduate into.
3. **Fixed prices, published.** Transparency is rare in SK (most agencies hide pricing) and is itself a differentiator. Fixed numbers, not ranges.

All prices EUR (quote CZK equivalent in Czech proposals). Net prices — as non-VAT-payer (živnosť under turnover threshold), prices are final, which undercuts VAT-registered agencies by 23% for small clients. Say this in proposals.

## 2. The CEE Price List (standard — after pilot slots fill)

### Rung 0 — AI Visibility Audit — **€90** (free headline finding in outreach)
A *report*, not a build. Test their site in ChatGPT/Perplexity/Claude + crawler + schema check. PDF, 10 findings, 48h.
- The DM hook is free: *"I asked ChatGPT about your company — here's what it said (nothing). Screenshot attached."* Full report €90, credited against any purchase.
- Purpose: **pipeline, not revenue.** Every audit ends in a proposal. Benchmark: audits sell €250–500; €90 is a deliberate door-opener.

### Rung 1 — AI Visibility Upgrade — **€890** (one-time, ~1 week)
*(formerly "Sprint" — renamed for clarity)*

**What it is, in one sentence:** a one-week, fixed-price service that takes a business's *existing* website — one you did **not** build — and upgrades it so AI systems can read, understand, and cite it. No rebuild, no design changes — a technical layer added on top of what they already have.

**Why it's a separate product:** normally Layer 03 only comes bundled into a full build, forcing a business to throw away their current site just to get the AI layer. The Upgrade **unbundles Layer 03** and sells it alone. The pitch becomes *"keep your site, I'll make it AI-readable in a week"* — a far easier yes, and every business with an existing site becomes a prospect.

**What you deliver (Layer 03 on someone else's site):**

| Deliverable | What it does |
|---|---|
| Schema.org / JSON-LD | Structured data telling AI what the business is, its services, FAQs (strong evidence behind it) |
| Answer-structured content | Rewriting the top ~5 pages so AI can extract clean answers (the part that actually moves citations) |
| AI crawler permissions | robots.txt allowing GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, etc. |
| llms.txt | Machine-readable site summary — a **bonus, not the headline** |
| Before/after report | Proof: the site tested in ChatGPT/Perplexity before vs. after |

**Honest framing (don't over-promise):** the Upgrade is *setup + measurement*, not a guarantee of citations. You make the site **eligible** to be read and cited and you measure the change — you do not promise "ChatGPT will recommend you next week" (that takes weeks-to-months and depends on the domain's existing authority). Sell the system and the measurement, like SEO has been sold for 25 years.

**Positioning note:** do **not** pin the value on llms.txt. Even the closest EU competitor (AEOScore) states llms.txt "is not yet an industry standard… should not be your primary focus." Lead with schema + answer-structured content + crawler access + the before/after report.

- Comparable: AEOScore "Quick Wins from €800"; Western AEO setup €1,500–3,000. €890 is the CEE-adjusted wedge. Sells especially well to *agencies* as white-label (they resell at 2×).

> **Optional entry tier — One-Pager — €590–790.** A single-page site (Layer 01 only). Fills the gap below Studio for the most price-sensitive first deals (SK landing pages run €290–590). Add when needed; not a core focus.

### Rung 2 — Website builds (Layer 01, Layer 03 readable always included)

| Tier | Price | Includes |
|---|---|---|
| **Studio** | **€1,490** | 5–7 day AI-built website + AI-readable layer (schema, llms.txt, crawlers) + strategy call + full AEO content optimization |
| **Premium** | **€2,990** | Studio **+ the AI Agent (Layer 02)** + custom design direction + 2 extra revisions + 1 month maintenance free |

**The difference between the two is one thing the client can see: Premium has the AI Agent.**
- Math is clean and honest: Premium €2,990 − Studio €1,490 = **€1,500 = the agent** (same as the standalone add-on below).
- Studio sits in the SK productized-studio band (€1,290–2,990); Premium aligns with SK top-tier builds (€3,890) while bundling a €1,490 agent — strong value.
- *(Merged from the old Starter €1,190 / Studio €2,190 split — the €1,000 gap was unsellable.)*

### Rung 3 — AI Agent add-on (Layer 02) — **€1,490 setup**
Embedded autonomous agent: lead qualification, knowledge base, booking, CRM, email notifications. For a site they already have (or already bought from you).
- **No mandatory monthly fee.** The agent burns Claude API tokens (~€30–200/mo by traffic) — billed at cost, or covered inside a retainer (§5). CEE competitors advertise "no monthly subscriptions"; don't fight that.
- CEE benchmarks: Lithuania €1,200–2,500; Bulgaria from €1,800. €1,490 is competitive.

### Rung 4 — MCP Integration (Layer 04 — AI-actionable) — **€1,890**

MCP server, agent-callable tools, discovery manifest, direct booking (Cal.com), payment links (Stripe). Lets *outside* AIs operate the site without a browser. **Tech/SaaS clients only — don't pitch to local SMEs** (they won't value or understand it).
- No CEE competitor sells this at all (Context Studios DE charges €10,000–25,000). €1,890 is priced to win lighthouse case studies; discount hard (even free) for the first one. Raise after 2–3 delivered.

### Rung 5 — Retainers (the MRR floor) — 2 tiers
| Plan | Price | Includes | For whom |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Care** | €99/mo | Keep what you bought running: site uptime, backups, updates, security — **or** agent monitoring + token management + small KB tweaks | Anyone who wants it hands-off |
| **AI Visibility** | €249/mo | Everything in Care **+** monthly AI-visibility tracking, re-optimization as AI changes, citation report | Clients who want their AI presence actively grown & proven |

- **AI Visibility is the product that carries the "visible to AI" promise** — honestly, because it's framed as ongoing work over time, not a one-time miracle.
- SK maintenance is €25–100/mo; Care at €99 is top-of-band, justified by the AI layer. AEO maintenance benchmark is €150–350/mo; €249 sits mid, room to rise.
- **3-month minimum, both plans.** No month-to-month.
- *(Killed the old Care+ €169 muddy middle, and folded the old €149/mo agent fee in here — one recurring relationship, one bill.)*

## 3. Pilot Program — the real launch pricing (first 3 clients)

With zero case studies, the discount is not generosity — it's payment for proof. Explicit, contractual, time-boxed:

| Pilot deal | Price (−40%) | They give back |
|---|---|---|
| AI Visibility Upgrade | **€490** | Named case study + testimonial + before/after metrics rights |
| Studio build | **€990** | Same + screen-recording rights for content |
| Premium build | **€1,790** | Same + a referral intro to 2 contacts |

- Pilot retainers at 50% for 3 months (Care €49, AI Visibility €129) — starts the MRR engine.
- Publicly framed: **"3 pilot slots — 2026"**. Scarcity is real (capacity is real), explains the price, protects the list.
- When slot 3 closes, **announce it** — the close is itself a post and a price-credibility signal.
- Pilot targets must be *strategic*: 1 agency (white-label channel), 1 tech/SaaS company (agent + MCP showcase), 1 visible local brand (recognition). A kebab-shop testimonial won't sell a €2,990 Premium build.

**Cold-sales sequence (important):** never cold-quote the standard list. Lead with the **free Audit → small paid pilot → case study → then list pricing** for warm/referred leads.

## 4. Terms & Rules

1. **50% deposit / 50% on delivery.** No work before deposit — pilots included.
2. **Never discount price — adjust scope.** Below budget? Remove pages, or start with the Upgrade. Pilots are the only exception, and they're a program, not a discount.
3. Revisions: 2 rounds included, then €190/round — stated in every proposal.
4. Rush delivery (48–72h): +40%.
5. Every proposal shows **all four layers with prices**, even when only one is bought — plants the upsell roadmap.
6. White-label for agencies: standard prices −15%, no public credit.

## 5. Path to €5K/month at CEE prices (honest math — a model, not a forecast)

| Month | Mix | One-time | MRR | Total/mo |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 2 pilots (Upgrade €490 + Studio €990) | €1,480 | €50 | ~€1,500 |
| 2 | Pilot Premium €1,790 + Upgrade €890 + Studio €1,490 | €4,170 | €250 | ~€4,400 |

| 4–6 | 1–2 builds + 1–2 upgrades/mo, retainers compound | €3,000–4,500 | €800–1,400 | €4,500–6,000 |

- Every input (deals closed/month) is **assumed, not validated** — treat as a target, not a promise.

- 12-month client LTV: Upgrade €890 → Studio €1,490 → Premium/agent → MCP €1,890 → AI Visibility €249/mo ≈ **€6,500–9,500**.

## 6. Sales framing per rung (what to actually say)

- **Audit → Upgrade:** "I asked ChatGPT about your company and it recommended your competitors — because it can't read your site. One week, €890, and it can."
- **Upgrade → Build:** "Your site is now readable by AI. It's still slow/dated for humans — here's what a rebuild fixes."
- **Studio → Premium:** "Your site captured 0 leads last night. Premium adds the agent that qualifies visitors and books meetings while you sleep."
- **Build → MCP:** (tech clients only) "AI agents are becoming buyers. Your site can be the first in your industry they can actually transact with."
- **Anything → AI Visibility retainer:** "AI search results shift monthly. This plan tracks the moment you lose a citation — and gets it back."
