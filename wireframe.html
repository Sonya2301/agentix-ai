Section 04 — How It Works Process — 5 Steps
How we work.

From briefing call to live website in 5–7 days. Every site ships with Layer 03 basics included as standard — no extra charge.

  • 01
    Client Briefing

    30-min Calendly call + Notion briefing form. We extract brand voice, goals, target audience, competitors, and tone. Every prompt we write comes from here.

  • 02
    AI Copy Generation

    AI writes all website text based on the brief — headlines, body copy, CTAs, meta descriptions. Takes 30 minutes. Most freelancers charge €500+ for this alone.

  • 03
    AI-Assisted Build

    We build the full website — design, layout, responsive development. A full 5-page site in 3–5 hours. You review, refine, and direct.

  • 04
    Visual Generation

    Custom hero images, product visuals, and section backgrounds generated in the client's brand. No generic stock photos. Every visual is unique.

  • 05
    Deploy + Full Setup

    Domain setup, Analytics, Search Console, sitemap, schema.org JSON-LD, AI crawler permissions, GDPR banner. Client goes live. Includes a Loom handover video.
